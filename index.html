Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups are these holiday packages for?

We offer packages for Children aged 8-12 and Teenagers aged 13-19, with content tailored to each age group.

What types of courses are available?

Our current holiday offerings include: Story Builders & Creative Crafts, AI & Digital Skills, Money Savvy Kids (for 8-12); and Creative Writing & Storytelling, AI & Digital Skills, Global Languages & Cultural Intelligence (French only).

What is the duration of each holiday package?

Each course package runs for the full month of August (August 4 - August 31, 2025), with specific class timings as outlined in our schedule to avoid overlaps.

Are the classes online or in-person?

All Learning World Holiday Skills Packages are digitally delivered, allowing students to participate from anywhere with an internet connection.

Can a child enroll in multiple packages?

Yes, a single child can enroll in multiple packages as long as the class timings do not overlap. Please refer to the Holiday Class Schedule for details.

Do students receive certificates?

Yes, students receive certificates upon completion of their chosen package.

What payment methods are accepted?

We process secure payments via Paystack, accepting various methods including card payments, bank transfers, and USSD.
